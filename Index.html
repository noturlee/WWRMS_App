WWRMS Tracker – Attendance & Site Presence App

WWRMS Tracker App Download

Use this app to log your attendance while on site. The app records when you log in and log off together with your GPS location.

Download WWRMS Tracker APK

After download, open the APK from your Downloads folder to install the app on your Android device.

How to install

  • Tap the Download WWRMS Tracker APK button above.
  • On the Google Drive page, tap the Download icon.
  • Once downloaded, go to your phone’s Files / Downloads folder.
  • Tap the APK file and follow the prompts to install.
  • If asked, allow installation from this source (for this one app).

How to use the app

  • Create an account using your employee number as your username.
  • Set a password of your choice and log in.
  • When you arrive on site, open the app and tap the switch/button to log in.
  • Leave it on green for the duration of your shift.
  • Just before leaving site to return to the hotel, open the app again and log off (switch returns to red).

Important

  • Only log in when you are physically on site.
  • Always log off at the end of the day before leaving.
